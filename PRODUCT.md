# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Riftbound collectors and players scanning real cards from a phone while sorting collections, checking prices, or preparing decks. They need a fast result from imperfect camera captures, not a catalog browsing experience.

## Product Purpose

Recognize a Riftbound card from its printed set code and collector number, resolve it against a maintained card catalog, and show a current Cardmarket price filtered by card language, seller country, and preferred price mode.

## Brand Personality

Precise, practical, focused. The product should feel like a reliable field tool for TCG players.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing landing-page patterns, decorative card grids, heavy fantasy styling, fake marketplace claims, and any flow that exposes marketplace credentials on-device.

## Design Principles

- Scan first, browse second.
- Show confidence and fallback paths clearly.
- Prefer official card data and traceable market data.
- Keep the mobile surface dense, readable, and usable one-handed.
- Make future collection, deck, and trading features fit the same card identity model.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, reduced-motion compatibility, and controls that remain usable outdoors on a phone screen.
